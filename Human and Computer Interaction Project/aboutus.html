About Us

Fancy Art is the world’s leading art gallery, connecting people with art and artists they love.
Fancy Art offers an unparalleled selection of paintings, drawings, sculpture and photography in a range of prices, and it provides artists from around the world with an expertly curated environment in which to exhibit and sell their work. Based in Jakarta, Fancy Art is redefining the experience of buying and selling art by making it easy, convenient and welcoming for both collectors and artists.

History

Fancy Art is established in 1990 by its founder Aryanto Andikusumo with the idea of sharing his love for art with the people in Indonesia.
The first Fancy Art gallery opened in Jakarta, to this day holds its name as the largest art gallery in Southeast-Asia exhibiting various types of art from ancient to modern and contemporary art. Throughout time, with the evolution of art itself, Fancy Art expand it galleries throughout the entire world with the purpose of connecting art and people.
